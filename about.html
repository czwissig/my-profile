Introduction

Calling all students to the most thrilling running event of the year!
The Annual Student Marathon returns to the iconic campus of Kedge Business School in the heart of Paris.
Get ready to lace up your shoes, hit the pavement, and immerse yourself in the excitement of endurance and camaraderie.

Event Details

  • Date: Saturday, May 15th, 2024
  • Time: 7:00 AM
  • Location: Kedge Business School, Paris
  • Distance: Full Marathon (26.2 miles) and Half Marathon (13.1 miles) options available
  • Registration Fee: $20 for Full Marathon, $15 for Half Marathon (Special student pricing)
  • Registration Deadline: April 30th, 2024
  • Registration Includes: Official race bib, commemorative t-shirt, finisher medal, post-race refreshments

Course Highlights

Experience the thrill of running through the captivating streets of Paris, starting and finishing at the prestigious Kedge Business School campus.
From the enchanting banks of the Seine to the iconic landmarks of the city, every step of the route promises an unforgettable journey.

Why Join

The Annual Student Marathon is not just a race—it's a chance for students to push their limits, bond with peers, and create lasting memories.
Whether you're a seasoned runner or new to the sport, this event offers an inclusive and supportive atmosphere for all students to thrive.
